What does the company do?

The company produces complex and resource-intensive simulations in various scientific fields such as:

  • Simulation of astronomical entities such as Supernovae, Nebulas, Quasars, and various stars
  • Examination of molecular structure and properties of proteins (Protein folding), DNAs, and hydrocarbon chains

Company Requirements

The company has 10 employees who work on the simulations. They require a high-end workstation, peripherals (listed in hardware requirements), fast connectivity to internal servers, and wifi.

The 2 managers need network access and wifi in their offices. Printers in every room.

The system administrator requires a workstation in the server room for quick physical and remote access for maintenance and troubleshooting of servers.
